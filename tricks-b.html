8 tricks · Updated weekly
✉️ Gmail

"Offer times you're free" — instant meeting booking

Drop clickable time blocks into an email. Recipients pick one and it books both calendars automatically. Ends the "does 2pm work?" ping-pong.

Easy ⏱ 3–5 min saved
  1. Open a draft or reply in Gmail.
  2. Click the Calendar icon in the bottom toolbar.
  3. Select "Offer times you're free" and drag your open slots.
  4. Click Insert into email.
🕐
NEW Gmail

Schedule Send — draft late, deliver at the right hour

Draft parent emails whenever you have the bandwidth; deliver when they'll actually read them. Protects your evenings without delaying work.

Easy ⏱ Preserves work-life boundary
  1. Compose your email.
  2. Click the arrow next to Send.
  3. Choose Schedule send → pick date/time.
📄 Docs

The @ menu — Docs' universal insert shortcut

Type @ anywhere in a Doc for instant access to tables, dropdowns, meeting notes, colleague chips, files. Skip the nested menus.

Easy ⏱ Countless clicks saved

Type @ anywhere, followed by:

  • @table — insert a table
  • @dropdown — status trackers
  • @meeting — auto-fill from Calendar
  • @name — insert a people chip
🎙️
NEW Docs

Voice typing — dictate observations and meeting notes

Capture classroom observations or meeting recaps on the walk back to your desk. Google's voice recognition is remarkably accurate and understands punctuation.

Easy ⏱ 3–5× faster than typing
  1. Tools menu → Voice typing (⌘+Shift+S).
  2. Click the microphone → speak naturally.
  3. Say "period," "comma," or "new paragraph" for punctuation.
📊 Sheets

Dot notation — extract emails and names from Smart Chips

Sheets Smart Chips are objects. Pull properties off them like code: =A2.email. No copy-paste, no manual data entry.

Intermediate ⏱ Eliminates copy-paste
=A2.email

Also try .name, .[file name], .attendees

🌐
NEW Sheets

GOOGLETRANSLATE() — bulk-translate family communications

Write once in English; render in Spanish, Vietnamese, Arabic — whatever your community needs. Perfect for outreach lists, permission slips, and signage.

Intermediate ⏱ Family comms at scale
=GOOGLETRANSLATE(A2,"en","es")

Codes: es (Spanish), vi (Vietnamese), ar (Arabic), fr (French). Use "auto" for source auto-detect.

🚀 AI Prompt

Reading-level translator — differentiate any text in seconds

Paste any dense text; get an accessible version at the grade band you need. Preserves core vocabulary. Works in Gemini, Claude, or ChatGPT.

Easy ⏱ 15+ min saved per text
"Rewrite this text at a 3rd-grade reading level. Maintain the key vocabulary words but shorten sentence structures."

NEW AI Prompt

IEP progress note drafter — strengths-based language, fast

Feed observation notes to Gemini; get IEP-appropriate first drafts. Saves the "how do I phrase this?" work. Always requires human review.

Intermediate ⏱ 20+ min per IEP note
"You are helping a special education teacher draft an IEP progress note. Given these observations: [paste], write a progress summary in professional, strengths-based language covering current performance, progress toward goal, next steps. Do not fabricate data."
