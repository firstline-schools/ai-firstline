Google Resources

Google's own curated resources for educators. Everything below is officially maintained by Google — start here for Workspace apps and Gemini in the classroom.

  • Google Workspace ↗

    The home for Google Workspace — Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Slides, Meet, Calendar, Vids, and Gemini. Start here.

  • Google Workspace for Beginners ↗

    Applied Digital Skills self-paced course for staff new to Google Workspace. Covers the essentials before diving into AI.

  • Google for Education Learning Center ↗

    The broader K-12 hub — training, resources, and product guides across Workspace and Google for Education tools.

  • Google AI Educator Series ↗

    Free structured training on Gemini for teachers — video modules, hands-on activities, and classroom application ideas. Highly recommended for anyone starting with AI in the classroom.

  • Google Support ↗

    Google's help catch-all — searchable articles across every Workspace app, Gemini, Vids, and beyond. Where to go if the curated resources above don't cover your question.

PlayLab Resources

PlayLab is a 501c3 nonprofit platform where educators and students build AI apps for classroom use — shaping AI on their terms. Rolling out at FirstLine fall 2026.

  • PlayLab — Home ↗

    About the mission, the platform, and how educators build AI apps for their classrooms.

  • PlayLab for Educators ↗

    Educator resources, community, and getting-started guides. Watch this space for FirstLine-specific rollout details this fall.

AI for Equity Resources

FirstLine is partnering with AI for Equity starting with senior leader development, expanding from there. Their tagline is "Innovate with Intent" — helping school systems implement AI equitably so it reduces, rather than widens, the equity gap.

  • AI for Equity — Home ↗

    Overview of programs — AI Leadership Accelerator, AI Literacy Accelerator, AI Vanguard Fellowship — and their approach to equitable AI in K-12.

  • AI-in-Action Video Library ↗

    Curated collection of AI use cases in classrooms — filterable by tool (Gemini, ChatGPT, others). Practical demos from real educators.

Note: the video library is hosted on Vercel — FLS's Securly filter may block it on the school network. If you see a filter page, ask Joe (IT) to whitelist ai-video-library.vercel.app.
