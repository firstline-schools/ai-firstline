P-007 · Live Oak Elem
Week 6 / 8

AI-Assisted Writing Feedback

HypothesisTeachers can give more specific feedback in less time using AI as a first pass.
Method6 teachers, parallel sections, weekly time logs + sample quality review.
ToolMagicSchool (approved)
Current Finding

Feedback time dropped 41% in week 4. Quality rating from peer reviewers went up, not down. Teachers report conferencing more.

P-009 · Arthur Ashe
Week 11 / 12

Custom GPTs for Differentiation

HypothesisCustom GPTs configured for grade level keep struggling students working independently longer.
Method3 teachers, 78 students, 6th grade math, observation + exit ticket analysis.
ToolSchoolAI (approved)
Current Finding

Independent work stretches up to 18 minutes (baseline 7). Students ask the bot for clarification before raising hands.

P-011 · Phillis Wheatley
Week 2 / 10

Lesson Planning from Standards Up

HypothesisStarting from the LA standard (not the textbook page) produces tighter, more aligned lessons.
Method4 teachers, 4 grade levels, blind review of pre/post lesson alignment.
ToolCustom prompt + Brisk (approved)
Early Signal

Too early to call. Teachers spending more time on the lesson, not less — but it's the right time, on alignment.

P-006 · Network
Concluded · Did not adopt

AI-Generated Reading Passages

HypothesisAI can generate reading passages at any Lexile level matched to student interest.
Method8 teachers, fluency + comprehension comparison over 6 weeks.
What We Learned

Lexile claims were inconsistent. Vocabulary was thin. Not adopting, but the failure mode is documented for next time.
